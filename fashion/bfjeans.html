How To Style Boyfriend Jeans

Ultra Femme

Rihanna puts a feminine twist on her baggy, oversized jeans with a ruffle-sleeved button-down and pointed pumps.

Boho Babe

Add an unexpected touch of bohemian flair to ripped and cuffed boyfriend jeans with a printed, billowy crop top, as demonstrated by Hailey Baldwin.

Polished Back

Elevate a pair of non-ripped boyfriend jeans for nighttime with a dressed-up blouse (like Zoe Kravitz's velvet v-neck) and classic black pumps.
